About

Uninstalls programs installed from SoftwareValet packages.

How To Use

Select a program from the list of installed packages, and click the Uninstall button.

Uninstallation

Yes, you can now use Uninstall to uninstall Uninstall. (It turns out you always could...) After you uninstall Uninstall, you can still use it untill you exit.
